Overview

At BizAI, we are committed to delivering high-quality AI tools and automation services to small businesses across India. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are granted, how to request one, and the timelines you can expect.

Our commitment If you are not satisfied with our service within the eligible refund window, we will process your refund — no questions asked.
2

Subscription Plans (BizAI Pro & Business)

BizAI offers monthly subscription plans billed in Indian Rupees (₹). The following terms apply:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Upon cancellation, your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • A full refund is available if requested within 7 days of the initial payment date.
  • Refunds are not provided for partial months or unused time after the 7-day window.
  • Renewals are non-refundable unless there is a technical issue on our end.
Example You subscribed on March 1st. If you request a refund by March 8th, you are fully eligible. After March 8th, no refund is provided for that billing cycle.
3

Agency Sprint Services

The Agency Sprint is a one-time, done-for-you automation build. The following terms apply:

  • A 50% refund is available if the project has not yet commenced (within 48 hours of payment).
  • Once work has begun, no refund is issued for completed deliverables.
  • If BizAI fails to deliver agreed-upon automations, a full or partial refund will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
  • Any disputes must be raised within 14 days of project completion.
4

Eligible Refund Situations

You are eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • You were charged in error (duplicate payment, wrong amount).
  • The service was not accessible due to a technical fault on our end for more than 48 continuous hours.
  • You requested cancellation within 7 days of your first subscription payment.
  • The Agency Sprint project was not started and you requested cancellation within 48 hours.
5

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at mintu73127@gmail.com with the subject line: "Refund Request — [Your Name]"
  2. Include your payment ID (found in your Razorpay receipt email).
  3. Briefly describe the reason for your refund request.
  4. Our team will review and respond within 2 business days.
Tip Keep your Razorpay payment confirmation email — it contains the Payment ID you'll need for your refund request.
6

Refund Processing Timeline

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
  • The amount will be credited back to the original payment method (UPI, card, or net banking).
  • Bank processing times may add an additional 2–3 business days depending on your bank.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.
7

Exceptions & Non-Refundable Items

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after the 7-day refund window has passed.
  • Failure to use the service during the subscription period.
  • Violations of our Terms of Service leading to account suspension.
  • Requests made after 14 days from the payment date (for subscriptions).
  • Completed Agency Sprint deliverables that meet the agreed scope of work.
8

Questions or Disputes

If you have any questions about this policy or want to raise a dispute, please reach out. We aim to resolve all issues fairly and promptly.
